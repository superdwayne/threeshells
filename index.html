How to Play

Welcome to the Game of Shells!

  • There are 3 shells and a hidden ball.
  • Watch carefully as the shells shuffle.
  • Click on the shell where you think the ball is hidden.
  • If you guess right, you earn points!
  • The game gets faster with consecutive wins. Try to score as much as you can!
